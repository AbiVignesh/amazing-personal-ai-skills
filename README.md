# amazing-personal-ai-skills

A collection of specialized AI skills designed to simplify your personal life. Each skill is optimized for a specific use case and works seamlessly with leading AI providers like Claude, Perplexity, VS Code, and more.

---

## Quick Start Guide to Skills

Choose the skill that matches your need:

### 1. 🏦 Bank Statement Categorizer

**What it does:** Analyzes bank statements and automatically categorizes every transaction for spending insights.

**When to use it:**
- You want to understand where your money is going
- Need to categorize expenses automatically
- Want to track spending patterns
- Preparing for budgeting or financial analysis

**How to use it:**
1. Upload your bank statement (PDF, CSV, or text format)
2. Mention the bank name (if not obvious)
3. The skill will extract all transactions and categorize them by expense type
4. Get a clean markdown table + downloadable CSV file
5. Use the categorized data to identify spending leaks and optimize budgets

**Best for:** Indian banks (ICICI, HDFC, Axis, SBI, Kotak, etc.), UPI transfers, and local merchants

### 2. 🎯 Career Guidance

**What it does:** Provides personalized career path recommendations based on your skills, experience, and aspirations.

**When to use it:**
- Deciding on your next career move
- Considering a career pivot or transition
- Want to advance in your current field
- Exploring opportunities at a new career stage
- Need to identify skill gaps for your target role

**How to use it:**
1. Share your resume (PDF, DOCX, or text) or describe your work experience
2. Tell the skill your current role, years of experience, and key skills
3. Mention your career goals and any constraints (location, work style, salary)
4. Get personalized analysis including:
   - Career options that leverage your strengths
   - Skill gap analysis for target roles
   - Transition timeline (if changing careers)
   - Professional development roadmap

**Best for:** Career changes, advancement planning, skill development, and market insights across all industries

### 3. 🏥 Lab Results Analyser

**What it does:** Interprets medical lab reports and provides clear, actionable health insights with visual indicators.

**When to use it:**
- You received lab test results and want to understand them
- Need to track health trends across multiple reports
- Want to understand what specific biomarkers mean
- Looking for a second opinion on test results
- Want to know which results need doctor follow-up

**How to use it:**
1. Upload your lab report (PDF, image, or CSV) OR manually enter values
2. Provide your age and sex for context-aware analysis
3. The skill will:
   - Classify each biomarker with traffic-light status (🟢 Normal / 🟡 Borderline / 🔴 Concerning)
   - Compare against age-sex-adjusted reference ranges
   - Track trends across previous reports (if available)
   - Generate an interactive HTML dashboard
   - Provide actionable recommendations

**Important:** ⚠️ This is for educational purposes only—always discuss results with your doctor

**Best for:** Understanding blood tests, cholesterol, blood sugar, CBC, thyroid, vitamins, hormones, and health tracking

---

### 4. 💰 Personal Finance Advisor (Maya)

**What it does:** Routes you to specialized sub-skills for comprehensive financial diagnostics and planning.

**When to use it:**
- Want a complete financial health assessment
- Need spending behavior analysis
- Want to review your investment strategy
- Planning for emergencies or stress testing your finances
- Looking for action plans and wealth building strategies
- Interested in global investing or income diversification

**How to use it:**
1. Share your financial situation with relevant data:
   - Monthly income (gross and after-tax)
   - Monthly fixed and variable expenses
   - Current savings and investments breakdown
   - Any liabilities (loans, EMIs)

2. Mention what you want to achieve (e.g., "How am I doing financially?" or "Create a plan for me")

3. Maya will route you to the right sub-skill:
   - **Financial Health Diagnosis** → Get a rating of your overall financial position
   - **Behavioral Spending Audit** → Identify where your money is actually going
   - **Investment Audit** → Review your portfolio allocation and investment choices
   - **Stress Test Scenarios** → Test how you'd survive job loss or market crashes
   - **Wealth Action Planning** → Get a 30/90/12-month roadmap to achieve your goals
   - **Global Investing Strategy** → Guidance on US stocks, crypto, international diversification
   - **Income Diversification** → Strategies for multiple income streams and side gigs

**Best for:** Holistic financial planning for Indian residents in INR, investment optimization, and wealth building

## Integration Guide

### For VS Code Users
Copy the skills folder into your VS Code prompts directory:
```
~/.vscode/extensions/copilot/
```

### For Claude/Other AI Providers
Use the skill SKILL.md files directly—copy the content and paste it into your AI chat context for specialized guidance.

### File Structure
Each skill folder contains:
- `SKILL.md` — Complete skill documentation with workflows, inputs, and outputs
- Additional reference files (diagrams, guides, templates) as needed

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Provide complete data** — The more details you share, the better the analysis
2. **Be specific about your goal** — Each skill adapts based on your intent
3. **Use document uploads** — PDFs and structured files yield better extraction than manual input
4. **Follow the suggested workflow** — Each skill has an optimized step-by-step process
5. **Ask for export formats** — Most skills can provide outputs as CSV, markdown tables, or interactive HTML
