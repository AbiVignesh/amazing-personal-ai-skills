## What Will Kill You in Interviews

❌ **Red flags that hurt:**

1. **Can't communicate clearly**
   - Impact: Fails communication round, coding round
   - Fix: Practice explaining out loud

2. **Slow on medium problems** (>50 min)
   - Impact: Won't finish problem
   - Fix: Speed drills on pattern you're weak in

3. **Missing edge cases**
   - Impact: Fails correctness
   - Fix: Always discuss edge cases at start

4. **No system design experience**
   - Impact: Fails design round (50% of interview)
   - Fix: Design 5 systems

5. **Bad behavioral stories**
   - Impact: Fails half or more of Amazon/Apple
   - Fix: Practice STAR method 5+ times each

---

## What Will Help You in Interviews

✅ **Green flags that help:**

1. **Clear communication**
   - Talk through your approach before coding
   - Explain edge cases you're handling

2. **Speed on medium problems** (35-40 min)
   - Shows confidence, leaves time to discuss

3. **Handling hints gracefully**
   - "Good point, here's how I'd adjust..."
   - Shows willingness to learn

4. **System design basics**
   - Can identify services, databases, bottlenecks
   - Can discuss tradeoffs

5. **Strong STAR stories**
   - Clear, concise, shows learning
   - Connects to company values
